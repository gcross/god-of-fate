A Successful Chase

Andrea chases the fairy for about an hour, starting to doubt whether this is such a good idea, when the fairy stops. She catches up to it and sees a Tigerlamp plant under it. Carefully, she reaches down and picks it. When she looks up, the fairy is gone.

She falls to her knees and thanks you for guiding her to the plant. She then gets up and starts heading back to her home.
