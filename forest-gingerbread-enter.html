Entering The Gingerbread House

Not knowing why she was doing this, Andrea enters the... cottage? The woman leads her to an oven. “Here, look inside.”

Andrea looks inside the oven and sees... little Elly? she screams, and faints.

Daylight awakens her. She looks around, but the gingerbread house is nowhere to be found.

She sobs -- there is no way that she will be able to make it home in time now.

You have reached the end.
